4-step process

Rides Lo rider signup process

Each step below can be reviewed and tracked in analytics so you can measure where potential riders stay engaged or drop off.

1
Account setup

Download the Rides Lo app and create your account.

  • Download the Rides Lo app.
  • Choose your language.
  • Enter your mobile number.
  • Set up a password or use OTP instead.
2
Personal details

Enter your profile and identity details.

  • First name and last name
  • Gender: male or female
  • Driving licence number
  • Email address
  • Current location
3
Vehicle details

Add your vehicle information.

  • Vehicle number
  • Date
  • Vehicle name or model
  • Vehicle color
4
Documents

Upload your required documents and finish signup.

  • Aadhaar card front and back
  • RC front and back
  • DL front and back

Final activation

Sign up, switch your status to online, and start getting rides.

Once your details and documents are accepted, complete signup in the app, set your rider status to online, and you can begin receiving rides.

Frequently asked by riders

Questions future rider partners usually ask.

Can I use OTP instead of setting a password?

Yes. The rider process includes the option to set a password or continue using OTP during account creation.

Which documents should I keep ready?

Keep Aadhaar front and back, RC front and back, and driving licence front and back ready before signup.

What happens after my profile is approved?

After signup approval, switch your rider status to online in the app and you can start receiving rides.
